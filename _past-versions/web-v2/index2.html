Welcome to SOCS

The Society of Computing Students at the University of Mount Union is an organization for students with interests in computing fields such as Computer Science, Information Systems, or Media Computing.

Web standards

Web standards are important, and the SOCS website adheres to them. If SOCS looks goofy in your browser, you're likely using a terrible browser that doesn't support web standards.
