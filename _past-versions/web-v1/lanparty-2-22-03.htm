LAN Party:

Feedback:

  • We want your ideas and suggestions about this LAN party!  Send us an email.

Quick Info:

  • When- Saturday, February 22nd at 7pm

  • Where- KHIC Room 13

  • How- Bring your whole setup, including monitor and cables

General Info:

  • We will be hosting the LAN Party in KHIC Room 13.  This is the first room on the right in the north side of KHIC, right before the language lab. It will run from 5pm to 4am.  Actually gaming won't start until 7pm.  The first couple hours will be for setting up the servers and networking.  If you would like to help setup, please come prior to 7pm.  

  • We will be playing Quake 3, MS Midtown Madness, and Starcraft.  Other games are welcome.  Because we are having the event in a room other than the CSIS lab, you must bring everything for your computer with you.  This includes your monitor, all your cables (power and network).  We will be connected to the campus network through a 10/100mbps (10mbps to the campus) switch.  This will allow students who want to play but don't want to lug all their equipment to enjoy the gaming.  Check this page for a list of servers.

  • We will be providing beverages.  We will be buying pizza around 9pm.  If you would like some, please bring money.
